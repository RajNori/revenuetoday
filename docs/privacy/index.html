Overview

Revenue Today (“the App”) is designed to keep your financial information on your device by default. This policy describes how information is handled when you use the App.

Data stored on your device

Revenue entries, summaries, preferences, and other content you enter are stored locally using Apple’s on-device storage (including Core Data where applicable). We do not operate our own servers to collect this data for the core functionality of the App.

Notifications

If you enable optional notifications (for example, weekly summaries or payment reminders), the App may schedule local notifications on your device. Granting notification permission is optional and controlled in iOS Settings.

Analytics and tracking

The App does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs to track you across apps and websites as part of this policy’s scope. If that changes in a future version, this page will be updated before such features go live.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. Do not use the App if you are not old enough to enter into terms in your region.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” note at the bottom reflects the latest version. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact options on the Support page.
