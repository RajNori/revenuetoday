Quick answers

  • Where is my data?
    Your entries stay on your iPhone or iPad. Sign in to iCloud backup if you use Apple’s device backup—per Apple’s policies.
  • Notifications not working?
    Open Settings → Notifications → Revenue Today and ensure alerts are allowed. Also check in-app notification toggles under Settings in the App.
  • Wrong totals or currency?
    Check your hourly rate and entry amounts in the app. Currency display follows your device locale where applicable.
